oneacre.online would like to announce a year of attempting to publish and distribute unprintable works.

Your purchase of a link will support artists who makes these works.

oneacre.online will move forward into being a space for unprintable text-based works, with a focus on showcasing writing of emerging and upcoming artists based in The Hague, The Netherlands. It will host four works throughout 2017-2018 in a three months cycle, with the first one being released in December 2017.

Programme updates, information and news will be announced through the website soon or you could sign up for our newsletter here, to have links arrive in your mailbox.

credits

oneacre.online is an experimental publication and distribution project, of size exactly equal to the area of 1 chain by 1 furlong that aims to utilise this online space to seed unprintable works.

Made by Stef Kors, Titus Knegtel, Victoria Douka-Doukopoulou and with the kind support of the Creative Industries Fund NL.
